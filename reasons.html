Jan Patrick's Love Letter
What is love? Love Letter Why do I love you?

REASONS WHY I LOVE YOU



First of all, you know who I am, through and through, and still love me for it. You accept me for who I really am. You make me laugh. You hold me in your arms, and say nothing more than that you love me. I love you for the look you give me. You're proud to be with me. You're a good listener. You always remind me that everything is going to be okay.

YOU MAKE ME HAPPY, MY LOVE!



My heart beats wildly for you. My pulse quickens whenever you are near. My brain churns constant thoughts of you, and my body aches for you. I simply can't help but react to you in every way. So, yes I love you. Not only do I love you. I want you. I desire you. I yearn you. Deeply. Constantly. Always.

Be completely humble and gentle; be patient, bearing with one another in love.


Ephesians 4:2
